## 💼 Research Associate (RA) & Research Assistant Pathways

If you want to gain additional publication credentials, work with top faculty before starting a PhD, or prefer a direct salaried research role, applying as a Research Associate (RA) or Research Assistant is an excellent pathway.

### 1. Singapore (NUS & NTU) — The Smart City Hub
Singapore is a premier global hub for urban mobility and AI research, heavily funded by the government's Smart Nation initiative.
- **Grab-NUS AI Lab:** Joint lab between Grab and NUS. Frequently hires RAs to work on real-world transportation/traffic datasets, routing algorithms, and spatio-temporal prediction.
- **NUS School of Computing:** Faculty regularly recruit RAs for spatio-temporal GNN projects.
- **NTU School of Computer Science & Engineering (SCSE):** Active research in intelligent transportation systems, traffic simulation, and GIS/spatio-temporal AI.
- **Why it fits:** Singapore offers a structured work pass for international RAs (who hold a Master's/M.Tech). It is a fantastic pipeline directly into their PhD programs.

### 2. Hong Kong (HKUST, HKU, PolyU) — The GNN Pipeline
Hong Kong universities use RA roles as their primary mechanism to evaluate international PhD students.
- **CityMind Lab (HKUST-GZ) & Urban AI Lab (HKUST):** Both Yuxuan Liang and Hui Xiong hire RAs. Working here for 6–12 months almost guarantees a transition to their PhD if your performance is strong.
- **Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU):** Outstanding groups in land surveying, geoinformatics, and urban transport.

### 3. Europe (Germany, Switzerland, Netherlands) — Salaried RA/PhD Positions
In continental Europe, a PhD student is officially employed as a **Research Assistant** or **Research Associate**. You do not pay tuition; instead, you get a full salary (e.g., TV-L E13 scale in Germany) with employee benefits.
- **TUM (Stephan Günnemann):** Continually hires PhD/RA candidates on a rolling basis.
- **TU Delft / Eindhoven University of Technology:** Heavy focus on smart grid, intelligent traffic networks, and GNNs.
- **ETH Zurich:** Highly prestigious research assistant positions in spatial and graph ML.

### How to Apply for RA Roles:
1. **Highlight PyTorch & Engineering Skills:** RA roles are funded by specific research grants. Professors need someone who can write clean, scalable PyTorch code and manage data pipelines *immediately* without training. Mention your M.Tech codebase and dataset experience (METR-LA, PEMS-BAY, transit data).
2. **University Job Portals:** Regularly search NTU Workday, NUS Careers, and HKUST vacancies.
3. **Cold Outreach:** Use the template below, modifying it to ask for RA opportunities.
